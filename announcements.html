General Rate Adjustment (GRA)

In order to provide you with reliable and continuous service we announce that “GENERAL RATE ADJUSTMENT (GRA)” will be implemented for all shipments from all Turkish ports to East-Mediterranean (TIES and TEL services) , West-Mediterranean(ASA and ITE services) and North Europe (NWC service) ports by starting 1st January 2024 (vessel departure dates will be the start date for application).


Turkon USA Line - General Rate Adjustment (GRA)

In order to provide you with reliable and continuous service we announce that “GENERAL RATE ADJUSTMENT (GRA)” will be implemented for all shipments from Turkiye, Egypt, Israel, Lebanon, Black Sea, and Spain to the USA ports by starting 21st January 2024 (vessel departure dates will be the start date for application).

European Union (EU) Emission Trading System

The EU ETS is a cornerstone of the EU's policy to combat climate change and its key tool for reducing greenhouse gas emissions cost-effectively. It is the world's first major carbon market and remains the biggest one. It is important to know that the EU ETS is not a sustainable product offered by Turkon.


USA Service-Peak Season Surcharge (PSS) Announcement

With the ongoing increase in export demand, in order to provide you with reliable and continuous service we announce that “PEAK SEASON SURCHARGE (PSS)” will be implemented for all shipments going to the USA ports by starting 24th November 2023.
