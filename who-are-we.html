who are we

We are sailors

As Turkon Line Family, we are passionate about blue and the sea. We draw strength from the Kalkavan Family's experience at sea and move forward with the experience of our deep-rooted history in the field of maritime transportation.

We are designers

We reveal the Turkon Line difference in maritime transportation. With our "Customer-Specific Solutions" service model, we offer different and flexible solutions to meet the needs of our customers and handle every stage of our service carefully. 

We are craftsmen

We process the details like a craftsman, with the meticulousness of a diamond master. We know the value you place on your products and the effort you put in, and we approach them with the meticulousness of a craftsman. We carry your loads carefully and safely, and we have been working continuously with the responsibility of your trust for 25 years.


Turkon Container Transport and Maritime Inc. (Turkon Line) was founded in 1997 by members of the Kalkavan Family. Turkon Line, which was established to provide container transportation to the USA, which has a very important place in world maritime trade, became the first Turkish company to provide direct service to the USA by providing the shortest transit time in its sector. 

Turkon Line has Country Directorates and offices in New York, Norfolk and Savannah in America, Hamburg in Germany, Antwerp in Belgium and Felixstowe in England, and in Turkey in Istanbul, Izmir, Mersin, Bursa and Ankara. provides services through regional directorates. In addition, it continues its activities in various parts of the world by providing service to 38 ports on 4 continents with 19 agencies and 37 agency offices in 23 countries.

Turkon Line, which actively provides container transportation services in the American, European, Mediterranean and Black Sea markets, is one of the leading brands in its sector. USA, Egypt, Israel, Spain, Germany, Lebanon, England, Benelux, Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, Ukraine, Romania, Georgia, Morocco, Portugal, Italy and France are among the countries it serves. While export transportation mostly includes chemicals, white goods, electronic goods, tobacco, paper, textile and carpet sectors, import transportation includes sectors such as automotive, cotton, paper and chemicals. With its direct and regular service, Turkon Line ships deliver the products they carry from Turkey to the East Coast of America, providing the shortest transit time in the industry. Turkon Line is among the market leaders in the Eastern Mediterranean, the East Coast of America and Turkey, which are the markets it focuses on.  Focusing on the

" Customer-Specific Solutions " service concept, Turkon Line increases the quality of service it provides day by day with its own offices and experienced team. In the developing and rapidly growing maritime transportation sector, it also offers technological options to its customers who demand "digital" service with its "Customer-Specific Solutions" service approach, which it has successfully implemented. 

Turkon Line offers integrated transportation services to its customers in every region of Turkey with the support it receives from Turkon Holding's land and railway transportation companies. In addition, the ships belonging to the Turkon Line fleet are produced at Sedef Shipyard, one of the largest private shipyards and group companies in Turkey in terms of capacity.
 
Turkon Line, which prioritizes sustainable development in order to preserve the ecological balance of the seas, carries out all its voyages with environmental awareness and a sense of responsibility towards nature. Turkon Line, which acts with the aim of efficient use of natural resources and prevention of pollution with the environmental policies it has adopted since its establishment, is also aware of its important role in combating the climate crisis. Within the framework of compliance with the IMO 2020 Regulation, Turkon Line broke new ground in its sector in Turkey by installing a Scrubber system on all its ships. While it carries out its innovation activities with environmental awareness, it also shapes the corporate culture with this understanding. Turkon Line improves itself day by day with its great love and gratitude to the seas and in line with its vision of environmentally friendly transportation. 

Turkon Container Transport and Maritime Inc.; It has ISO 27001 Information Security Management System, ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System, ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management System and ISO 10002 Customer Satisfaction Management System certificates.

Turkon Container Transport and Maritime Inc. is the first and only Regular Overseas Container Transportation Line included within the scope of the Turquality Brand Support Program.
